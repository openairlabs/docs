# Flyweel Quickstart Guide

<Callout type="info">
**Get up and running in under 10 minutes** - This guide covers everything you need to start optimizing your ad campaigns with Flyweel's SpendOps platform.
</Callout>

Welcome to **Flyweel** - the intelligent SpendOps platform that eliminates advertising waste and maximizes your campaign performance. This comprehensive guide will walk you through the essential steps to get your account set up and start analyzing your ad campaigns across Google Ads and Meta Ads.

## What You'll Accomplish

By the end of this guide, you will have:

- ✅ **Completed your business profile** for personalized recommendations
- ✅ **Connected your Google Ads and Meta Ads accounts** for unified campaign analysis  
- ✅ **Accessed your unified dashboard** with cross-platform performance insights
- ✅ **Started using the AI agent** for campaign optimization recommendations

## Step 1: Complete Your Business Profile

The first step is to tell us about your business. This helps Flyweel provide personalized insights and benchmarks relevant to your industry and scale.

### Required Profile Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Company Details">
    - **Company Name** *(Optional)*: Enter your business name for personalized reporting
    - **Country**: Select your primary market for localized insights and benchmarks
    - **Industry**: Choose your business sector for relevant optimization recommendations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Advertising Context">
    - **Monthly Ad Spend** *(Optional)*: Your estimated monthly advertising budget across all platforms
    - **How did you hear about us?**: Help us understand how you discovered Flyweel
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
**Pro Tip**: You can skip this step initially and complete it later in your **Account Settings**, but providing this information upfront enables more accurate benchmarking and recommendations.
</Callout>

---

## Step 2: Connect Your Advertising Platforms

After completing your profile, you'll be directed to the **Connections** page. This is where the magic happens - connecting your ad accounts enables Flyweel to provide unified analytics and cross-platform optimization insights.

### Connecting Google Ads

Google Ads integration provides access to your Search, Display, Shopping, and YouTube campaign data.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Connection">
    Click the **Connect Google Ads** button to begin the authentication process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate & Authorize">
    You'll be redirected to Google's secure authentication page. Log in with the Google account that has access to your Google Ads accounts and grant Flyweel permission to access your campaign data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Accounts">
    Once authenticated, you'll see your complete account hierarchy including:
    - **Individual Google Ads accounts**
    - **Manager accounts (MCC)** with their sub-accounts
    - **Account status indicators** (Active, Suspended, etc.)
    
    Select the accounts you want to analyze by checking the boxes next to them. Selecting a manager account automatically includes all sub-accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save & Import">
    Click **Save & Refresh** to save your selections and trigger the initial data import. This process typically takes 2-5 minutes depending on your account size.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Connecting Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

Meta Ads integration includes Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and Audience Network campaigns.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Connection">
    Click the **Connect Meta Ads** button to start the Meta authentication flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate & Authorize">
    You'll be redirected to Facebook's authentication page. Log in with the Facebook profile that has admin access to your ad accounts and grant Flyweel the necessary permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Ad Accounts">
    After authentication, you'll see a list of all Meta Ads accounts you have access to. Select the accounts you want to include in your Flyweel analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save & Import">
    Click **Save & Refresh** to save your account selection and begin importing your Meta campaign data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
**Data Import Time**: Initial data synchronization can take 5-15 minutes for large accounts. You'll receive an email notification when your data is ready for analysis.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Explore Your Unified Dashboard

Once your ad accounts are connected and data is imported, you'll have access to Flyweel's powerful unified dashboard that combines performance data from all your connected platforms.

### Dashboard Overview

Your main dashboard provides:

- **Cross-platform performance metrics** with unified KPIs
- **Spend efficiency analysis** highlighting optimization opportunities  
- **AI-powered insights** and actionable recommendations
- **Real-time campaign status** across all connected accounts

### Key Features to Explore

1. **[AdGrid Campaign Manager](/features/adgrid-campaign-manager)** - Your central hub for viewing and managing campaigns across all platforms
2. **[AI Agent](/features/ai-agent-chat)** - Natural language interface for campaign analysis and optimization recommendations  
3. **[Reports & Analytics](/features/reports-analytics)** - Visual performance dashboards with customizable metrics

### Getting Started with the AI Assistant

The AI agent is immediately available on your dashboard. Try asking:

- *"What was my total spend across all platforms last month?"*
- *"Which campaigns are underperforming and need attention?"*
- *"Show me opportunities to improve my return on ad spend"*
